about Dubai

Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping,
ultramodern architecture and a lively nightlife scene.

History

Founded in the 1800s as a fishing village, Dubai has emerged as a major center for
regional and international trade since the early 20th century.

Population

Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates and the capital of the Emirate of Dubai.
