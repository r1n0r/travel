about Istanbul

Istanbul is a major city in Turkey that straddles Europe and Asia across the Bosphorus Strait.

History

Istanbul is an ancient city with a history that dates back over 2,600 years. Before being named Istanbul,
it was known as Byzantium and later Constantinople

Population

The city has a population of over 15 million residents, comprising 19% of the population of Turkey, and is
the most populous city in Europe.

Things To Do

Istanbul, the city where East meets West, offers a wealth of attractions and activities that showcase
its rich history, diverse culture, and vibrant atmosphere.

Places to Visit

Taksim Square
The Blue Mosque
